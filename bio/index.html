Biography

Boa Ho Man is currently a software engineer with Pocketmath, a self-serve mobile advertising platform. He writes code in a number of languages including Ruby, JavaScript, Golang, Java and Python.

He studied Computer Science at National University of Singapore and graduated with honors in 2011. With encouragement, he spent the next two years on an adventure to create a BI-charting SaaS that was eventually sunsetted. Since, he has continued full-stack web development at a number of high-growth start ups such as Wego and honestbee.

He is passionate about software engineering and artificial intelligence (mainly applied machine-learning) and intends to further his technical knowledge in these fields with a master's degree in the near future.
